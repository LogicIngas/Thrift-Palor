Store Policy

Returns and Exchanges ▼

We want you to be happy with your vintage truck parts. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied, you can request a return for store credit only within 30 days of receiving your order.

Returns must be in the same condition as when you received them. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will issue you store credit for the purchase price (excluding original shipping costs).

Shipping and Delivery ▼

We currently offer delivery only within South Africa.

We aim to dispatch orders within 2-3 business days after payment is confirmed.

Delivery times will vary depending on your location. You will receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the weight and dimensions of your order.

Payment ▼

We accept credit cards and secure online payment methods.

All prices include 15% VAT.

Payment must be made in full before your order is processed.
