Who We Are?

Thrift Palor is a vintage fashion e-commerce store dedicated to bringing you the best pre-loved clothes at affordable prices. Our mission is to promote sustainable fashion by giving high-quality garments a second life. We believe in style, quality, and a better planet. From timeless classics to modern treasures, every piece in our collection is carefully selected and inspected to ensure it meets our standards.


Creating beautiful and sustainable clothes that will last a lifetime.
