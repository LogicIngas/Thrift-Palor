About Us

Welcome to Thrift Palor! We are a passionate team of six developers who came together for our Project 2 assignment to create an online selling platform that addresses a real need in our community.

While exploring the world of online shopping, we realized there was a missing app that could make buying and selling second-hand items easier, safer, and more accessible for everyone. Our goal is to empower our community to connect, trade, and support sustainable living through a user-friendly web application.

Thrift Palor is more than just a project—it's our contribution to making local commerce better for everyone. We hope our platform helps you find great deals, declutter your space, and connect with others in your area.
